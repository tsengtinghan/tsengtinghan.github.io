about me

18, building for the singularity
I’m interested in ai, bio-inspired computing, and most of the emerging tech/science developments.

🌉 love startups, cities, long articles, ocean blue, and night sky

I grew up in Hsinchu, Taiwan, a city where the wind never stops blowing. The thriving semiconductor industry there inspired me to pursue a path in STEM, so I picked up C++ upon entering high school. I later became interested in machine learning and started working on side projects and conducting research by reading papers and watching tutorials on YouTube. Science fairs and various competitions made up a significant part of my high school, and they helped me realize what I truly wanted to do in the future—address societal problems with technology and generate wealth from it. Besides writing code, I sometimes write prose and poetry as a window out of my chaotic life.

這時，一陣令人陶醉的微風吹來，霧完全散了。天空中，夏夜燦爛的星海一望無際地顯現出來，在遠遠的山下，泰安的萬家燈火也形成了另一片小小的星海，彷彿是前者在一個小湖中的倒影。

林雲用她那輕柔的聲音吟誦起那首詩：

“遠遠的街燈明了，

好像是閃著無數的星星。

天上的明星現了，

好像是點著無數的街燈。 “

我跟著吟下去：

“我想那飄渺的空中，

定然有美麗的街市。

街市上陳列的一些物品，定然是世上沒有的珍奇。

……”

我的眼淚湧了出來。這美麗的夜中世界在淚水中抖動了一下又變得比剛才更加清澈。我明白自己是一個追夢的人，我也明白在這個世界上，這樣的人生之路是何等的險惡莫測，即使那霧中的南天門永遠不出現，我也將永遠攀登下去——

我別無選擇。

                                  ——《球狀閃電》，劉慈欣
